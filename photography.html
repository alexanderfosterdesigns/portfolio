Image-making with shadow, texture, and stillness.

The gallery keeps the quieter, cinematic mood from the luxury prototype, but the layout is cleaner and better separated from the rest of the portfolio. That makes the image work feel more intentional and more expensive.

Why this page matters

The portfolio can now breathe between mediums.

Photography usually loses impact when it is squeezed between design cards and product mockups. Giving it a dedicated page solves that problem and makes the whole site feel more curated.

Visual payoff
Stronger mood retention

Visitors stay inside one emotional register for longer, which helps the photography feel authored and helps the wider portfolio feel more controlled.

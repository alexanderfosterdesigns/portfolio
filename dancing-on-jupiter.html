Premise

A book built around emotional gravity: attraction, drift, distance, and the impossible feeling of trying to stay grounded while your life keeps moving faster than your body can process.

Atmosphere

Cold light, lift-off energy, loneliness, and the weird beauty of watching relationships behave like weather systems in space.

Why it matters here

It gives the writing side of the portfolio a sharper, more cinematic identity and pairs naturally with the dark interactive design language of the site.

Excerpt tone
"Some nights it felt less like falling in love and more like entering an orbit you were never built to leave."
Positioning inside the portfolio

This page is designed to sell the book as part of a wider creative practice. It sits alongside the apps and photography without shrinking to the level of a simple description card.

VERVE

  • Ad-free listeningCleaner user experience and better focus on the music itself.

App two

CHERIFLIX

A Netflix-like streaming app using streaming APIs and the TMDB database. The design leans into a more cinematic UI language to match the content-first nature of the app.

  • Streaming-first experienceBuilt around browse, discover, and play as the core interaction loop.
  • TMDB-backed discoveryUses real metadata to make the product feel more complete and believable.
  • Premium dark interfaceThe portfolio presentation reinforces the same mood the app itself needs.
Highlight to fix
App three

BOOKLI

A lighter, weirder, more modern alternative to Word. It lets users highlight text to get Claude or ChatGPT to repair or sharpen it, flags likely issues, and helps auto-format manuscripts for publishing.

  • Highlight-to-fix workflowFast interaction for editing instead of bouncing between windows and tools.
  • AI issue suggestionsPushes beyond spelling checks into structural and stylistic improvement.
  • Publishing formattingPositions the app as a real tool for authors instead of a novelty editor.

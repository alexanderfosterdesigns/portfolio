Premise

A coming-of-age story built from heat shimmer, friendships, mistakes, music, and the strange way one season can become a permanent reference point for the rest of your life.

Atmosphere

Streetlight glow, car windows down, late afternoons that stretch forever, and the emotional distortion that memory adds once enough time has passed.

Why it matters here

It gives the writing portfolio contrast. Next to the cosmic pull of Dancing on Jupiter, this book feels more intimate, nostalgic, and human-scaled.

Excerpt tone
"By August the light had changed, but none of us were ready to admit the summer was already turning into memory."
Positioning inside the portfolio

This page pushes the writing section beyond simple genre labels. The goal is to make the books feel like fully considered creative objects, with distinct emotional identities and their own pages to prove it.
